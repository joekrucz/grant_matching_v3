About Trellis

Our Mission

Trellis is an intelligent platform designed to help UK companies discover and secure the funding opportunities that best match their needs. We believe that finding the right grant shouldn't be a time-consuming challengeâ€”it should be straightforward, efficient, and tailored to your company's unique profile.

Our mission is to democratize access to UK funding opportunities by aggregating grants from multiple providers and using artificial intelligence to provide intelligent matching and analysis.

What We Do

Trellis aggregates funding opportunities from leading UK grant providers, including:

  • UKRI (UK Research and Innovation)
  • NIHR (National Institute for Health and Care Research)
  • Catapult Network
  • Innovate UK

We then use advanced AI technology to match these opportunities with companies based on their:

  • Company profile and business information
  • Technology readiness level (TRL)
  • Industry and SIC codes
  • Funding search criteria and preferences
  • Eligibility requirements

Key Features

Grant Aggregation

We continuously collect and update grant information from multiple UK funding providers, ensuring you have access to the latest opportunities.

AI-Powered Matching

Our intelligent matching system analyzes your company profile and identifies grants that align with your business needs and eligibility.

Company Management

Easily manage multiple companies, integrate with Companies House data, and track funding searches and grant matches.

Eligibility Analysis

Comprehensive eligibility checklists help you understand which grants you qualify for before you apply.

Our Technology

Trellis is built using modern, secure technology to ensure reliability and performance:

  • Django Framework: Robust backend infrastructure for secure data handling
  • PostgreSQL Database: Reliable data storage and management
  • AI Integration: Advanced AI for intelligent grant matching and analysis
  • Real-time Updates: Automated scraping keeps grant information current
  • Secure Infrastructure: Industry-standard security practices to protect your data

Data Sources

We aggregate grant information from official public sources and integrate with trusted data providers:

  • Grant Providers: Official websites and APIs from UKRI, NIHR, Catapult, and Innovate UK
  • Companies House: Public company information and filing history
  • 360Giving: Historical grant data for companies that have received funding

All data is collected from publicly available sources and updated regularly to ensure accuracy.

Privacy and Security

We take your privacy and data security seriously. Trellis is designed with privacy and security at its core:

  • Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • We comply with UK GDPR and data protection regulations
  • You have full control over your data and can delete your account at any time
  • We never sell your personal information
  • Secure authentication and session management
